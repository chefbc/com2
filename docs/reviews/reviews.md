## Michael Robertson ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Rating:** 5/5  
**Date:** 7 months ago  

I highly recommend Speckless Auto Spa.

I was recently offered a Toyota Sienna that had a serious interior diesel fuel smell—turns out a can of diesel had leaked in the back two years ago and soaked into the rear folding seats, carpet, and fabric. When I brought the Sienna to Kale, he used his equipment and an enzyme treatment to do a preliminary gas clean-up. He made real progress, but because the fuel had been sitting for two years—without the car being driven at all for the past year—the smell has spread to every inch of the vehicle and could not be completely removed.

What stood out most to me was Kale’s honesty and professionalism. He explained that had he been contacted at the time of the original spill, he likely could have fully resolved the issue. However, given the length of time the diesel had been left to soak into the vehicle’s interior, he was upfront in saying that a complete removal of all gas smell was no longer feasible—and, for that reason, he respectfully declined the job. That level of integrity is uncommon.

Kale demonstrated clear expertise and a straightforward approach, both of which I greatly respect. I will absolutely be using his services for my other vehicles and would not hesitate to recommend him and Speckless Auto Spa….And I will warn everyone to clean-up a gas spill right away.
