# Who we are

Speckless Auto Spa is your vehicle preservation and restoration specialist in **Waukesha, WI**, serving the greater Milwaukee and Waukesha area. We exist to give your car a showroom shine and keep it that way—whether you drive a daily commuter or a weekend show car.

## Our mission

We believe in **quality over quantity**. Instead of rushing vehicles in and out, we take the time to assess each car’s condition and recommend only what it needs. That means honest advice: we won’t sell you a full paint correction or ceramic coating if a solid detail is enough. Your time and budget matter as much to us as the result.

## What we do

- **Paint correction** — Removing swirls, scratches, and oxidation so your paint looks like new and is ready for long-term protection.
- **Ceramic coatings** — We are an authorized detailer for **Dr. Beasley’s** and use their small-batch, in-house formulations for lasting gloss and protection.
- **Detailing** — Full interior and exterior packages (Silver, Gold, Platinum) so you can choose the level that fits your vehicle’s condition and your goals.

Every job is done in a **safe, controlled environment** by detailers who treat your car like their own. From assessment to finish, we focus on clarity, consistency, and results you can see.
