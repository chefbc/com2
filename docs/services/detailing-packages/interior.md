# Interior Detailing Packages

Please specify if you would like interior, exterior, or both when booking.

We offer three interior packages: **Silver** (Express), **Gold** (Maintenance), and **Platinum** (Enhancement). Choose the level that matches your vehicle's condition.

---

## Silver Package — Interior

*Good condition.* For vehicles that are relatively clean with no stains, no pet hair, and no excessive dirt or dust.

- Interior Blow Out
- Heavy Seat & Carpet Vacuum
- Thorough Interior Wipe Down
- Door Panels Cleaned
- Center Console Cleaned
- Nooks & Crannies Cleaned
- Dash Cleaned
- Vents Cleaned
- Glass Cleaned
- Interior Sealant
- Odor Eliminator
- Door Jambs Cleaned

---

## Gold Package — Interior

*Moderate condition.* For vehicles that are dirty with some food crumbs and minor stains.

- Interior Blow Out
- Heavy Seat & Carpet Vacuum
- Light Shampoo on Carpets & Upholstery and/or Steam Cleaned
- Thorough Interior Wipe Down
- Door Panels Cleaned
- Center Console Cleaned
- Nooks & Crannies Cleaned
- Dash Cleaned
- Vents Cleaned
- Glass Cleaned
- Leather Conditioned
- Interior Sealant
- Odor Eliminator
- Door Jambs Cleaned

---
