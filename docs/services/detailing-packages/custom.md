# Custom detailing

Not every job fits a standard package. Whether you need a **one-off service**, **add-ons** to a Silver/Gold/Platinum detail, or a **tailored plan** for a specific problem, we can put together a custom scope.

## What we can do

- **Targeted interior work** — Pet hair removal, stubborn stains, odor treatment, or a single area (e.g. seats only, cargo area).
- **Exterior add-ons** — Engine bay only, headlight restoration, trim restoration, or extra decontamination.
- **Combinations** — Mix interior and exterior at different package levels, or add custom items to any package.
- **Special requests** — Classic cars, pre-sale prep, or “just make this part perfect.” Tell us your goal and we’ll recommend steps and pricing.

## How it works

Describe what you want—by phone, [contact form](../../contact.md), or in person. We’ll confirm the scope and give you a quote before any work starts. No surprises, no unnecessary upsells.
