# Full detailing packages

Our detailing services cover **exterior**, **interior**, **engine bay**, and **custom** options. You choose the level that matches your vehicle’s condition—**Silver** (Express), **Gold** (Maintenance), or **Platinum** (Enhancement)—and whether you want interior, exterior, or both.

## Choose your focus

- **[Exterior](exterior.md)** — Hand wash, wheels, glass, sealant, and more. Silver for regular maintenance, Gold for a deeper clean, Platinum for gloss and shine restoration.
- **[Interior](interior.md)** — Vacuum, wipe-down, upholstery and carpet care, leather conditioning. Silver for clean vehicles, Gold for moderate wear, Platinum for heavy soiling and stains.
- **[Engine bay](engine.md)** — Degreasing, cleaning, and dressing. Available as an add-on or standalone.
- **[Custom](custom.md)** — Need something specific? Pet hair, stains, one-off areas, or a tailored combo? We’ll build a plan for you.

## Silver, Gold & Platinum

Every package tier (Silver, Gold, Platinum) is available for exterior and/or interior so you can mix and match. See the [Exterior](exterior.md) and [Interior](interior.md) pages for full lineups, or view our [Packages](../../packages.md) page for a quick overview of all three tiers.

**When booking, specify interior, exterior, or both.**
