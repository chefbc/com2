# Engine Bay Detailing

Engine bay cleaning and detailing is available as an add-on or standalone service.

A clean engine bay helps with resale value, makes it easier to spot leaks and wear, and keeps your vehicle looking cared-for under the hood.

**Typical engine bay service may include:**

- Degreasing and cleaning of engine and bay surfaces
- Dressing of plastic and rubber components (where safe)
- Removal of leaves, debris, and light corrosion buildup

Contact us for availability, pricing, and to combine engine bay service with any [exterior](exterior.md) or [interior](interior.md) package.
